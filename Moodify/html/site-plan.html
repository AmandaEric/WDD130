Overview

Purpose

To help users become proactive with their mental health and motivate them as they try to build positive habits. Tracking moods visually makes it easier to recognize patterns, while habit tracking provides motivation to stay consistent.

Audience

Individuals trying to better them selfes in all aspects of their lives.
